Step 1: Create Formspree Account

Go to formspree.io and create a free account.

Step 2: Create a New Form

After signing up:

  1. Click "New Form"
  2. Name it "Oppfinity Payment Notifications"
  3. Copy the form endpoint URL

Step 3: Update the Code

Replace the Formspree endpoint in your Netlify function:

File: netlify/functions/process-payment-success.js

Line 35: Update the EMAIL_ENDPOINT variable with your Formspree URL

const EMAIL_ENDPOINT = 'https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID';

Step 5: Firebase Service Account

For Firebase updates, you'll need to add your Firebase service account credentials to Netlify environment variables:

  • FIREBASE_TYPE
  • FIREBASE_PROJECT_ID
  • FIREBASE_PRIVATE_KEY_ID
  • FIREBASE_PRIVATE_KEY
  • FIREBASE_CLIENT_EMAIL
  • FIREBASE_CLIENT_ID
  • FIREBASE_AUTH_URI
  • FIREBASE_TOKEN_URI
  • FIREBASE_AUTH_PROVIDER_X509_CERT_URL
  • FIREBASE_CLIENT_X509_CERT_URL
